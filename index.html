    ALT MODES
    ♦

    ENDLESS

    One room. Infinite waves. Survive as long as possible and collect item rewards between each wave.

    ☠

    BOSS RUSH

    Face all four bosses back to back. Collect loot between each fight. Defeat God to win.

    ⚿

    TREASURE HUNT

    Kill the vault boss, take its key, then fight back through new monsters and explosive traps. Return to the entrance to reveal a ladder or key chest.

    ♥

    CO-OP

    Two players, one screen. P1: WASD + mouse / Gamepad 1. P2: IJKL + U/; / Gamepad 2. Clear the dungeon together.

    ⚔

    PVP

    Two players fight each other in an arena. First to land 3 kills wins. P2 uses IJKL plus U/O/P/;.

    ⚖

    PRACTICE

    Spawn any enemy or challenge trial at will. Test builds and learn attack patterns without consequences.

    ◈

    CHALLENGE TESTING

    Enter a portal hub containing every challenge trial. Cleared rooms open a return portal to the hub.

    Comfort

    Reduce flashing Stops pulsing edge glow, hit flash, and CSS animations. Recommended for photosensitivity.
    Reduce motion Skips floor transition animations and portal pulse effects on the canvas.

    Screen shake Camera shakes on hits and explosions. Disable if this causes discomfort.
    Controller rumble Vibrate the controller on hits and explosions. Chrome/Edge only; no effect without a supported gamepad.
